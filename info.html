Adobe Flash Preservation

Information About This Site

This website is dedicated to preserving Adobe Flash Player and providing access to its installer for users who wish to play Flash-based content.

Adobe Flash Player was once an essential plugin for viewing multimedia on the web, but Adobe officially ended support for it on December 31, 2020. This site aims to help preserve the Flash legacy by making the installer available for download.

For more information on Adobe Flash Player and its discontinuation, visit Adobe's official page.

Please note: I am not affiliated with Adobe, and all credits for Flash Player go to the Adobe team. This site is a tribute to their work.
